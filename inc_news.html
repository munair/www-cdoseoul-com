The Gold Standard of the Capoeira Experience

For the last six years, Professor Zumbi and his students have been living a Capoeira experience that is unparalleled in Korea. Visit us on weekdays to partake in our 7:30 PM class rodas and prove it to yourself. The roda is always free.

Thanks to Mestre (Master) Suassuna, the founder of the Cordão de Ouro group in Brazil, Mestre Chicote, and a few other execptional teachers of this Afro-Brazilian artform, our group in Seoul delivers a Capoeira experience of the highest quality. To understand what is meant by the Capoeira experience we recommend that you join the group for a few months. The Capoeira experience is most easily documented via the roda. Nonetheless, Zumbi and his students continue living the Capoeira experience long after the class is over and all the games have stopped.
